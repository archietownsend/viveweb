## The short version

- You sign in with **Sign in with Apple**. You can choose to hide your email, and
  we never see your Apple password.
- Your account data — your profile, meal log, weight, and the health metrics used
  to calculate your scores — is stored securely in **your account on our servers**
  so your history is preserved and stays in sync.
- When you scan or compare a food, the photo or text you submit, plus a short
  summary of your profile, is sent to our processing service to generate a result.
  It is not used for advertising and not sold.
- We do not sell your data, share it with data brokers, or use it for advertising.

## Your account (Sign in with Apple)

Vive uses Sign in with Apple to create and secure your account. Apple shares a
unique identifier with us and, depending on your choice, either your email or a
private relay address that forwards to you. We use this only to identify your
account, store and sync your data, and contact you about the service. We never
receive your Apple ID password.

## What we store in your account

To provide the app across sessions and devices, we store the following in your
account on our servers:

- Your dietary profile, goals, conditions, and allergy information.
- Your meal log, food scans, and saved items.
- Your weight log and the health metrics used to calculate your daily scores and
  trends (for example sleep duration, Zone 2 minutes, resting heart rate, and
  heart rate variability).

This data is associated with your account identifier, transmitted over encrypted
connections, and protected by access controls. We retain it while your account is
active.

## Apple Health (HealthKit) data

With your permission, Vive reads heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate
variability, sleep, and workout data from Apple Health to show wellness trends and
estimate Zone 2 minutes.

- The metrics needed to calculate and show your scores are stored in your account
  as described above.
- We **never** use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing, never sell it, and
  never share it with data brokers.
- We do not store HealthKit data in iCloud.
- You can revoke access any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

This use complies with Apple's HealthKit requirements.

## Food scans and comparisons

To analyse a food, label, menu, or comparison, the **image or text you provide**
is sent to our backend, which forwards it to our AI analysis provider
(**Anthropic**, via the Claude API) to produce a result returned to your device.
A condensed summary of your profile (diet, conditions, allergies, goals, age) is
included so the verdict can be personalised. We do not use this content to train
models, and we do not sell it.

## Service providers

We share data only with providers that help us run the service, under agreements
that limit their use of it:

- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple and Apple Health.
- **Anthropic** — AI analysis of food scans and digests.
- Our cloud hosting and database provider, which stores your account data on our
  behalf.

## What we do not collect

We do not collect your contacts or precise location, and we do not use
third-party analytics or advertising SDKs or ad identifiers.

## Your choices

- Delete your account and its data from within the app (Settings → Delete
  account).
- Revoke Health, Camera, or Photo access in iOS Settings at any time.
- Stop sharing with Sign in with Apple from your Apple ID settings.
- Contact {% include email.html %} with any privacy request, including access or
  deletion.

## Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. When you delete your
account, we delete the associated data from our active systems, except where we
must retain limited records to comply with law.

## Security

Data sent between the app and our servers travels over encrypted connections
(HTTPS), and account data is protected by access controls. No method of
transmission or storage is perfectly secure.

## Children

Vive is not directed to children under 13. Do not use it if you are under that
age.

## Medical disclaimer

Vive provides general nutrition and wellness information only, generated in part
by AI which can be wrong. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical
advice, diagnosis, or treatment. See the in-app disclaimers and Terms.
