Everything you need to eat and live better

Scan any meal, get an honest verdict

Point your camera at a plate, a label, or a menu. Vive analyses it and returns a clear GOOD / MIXED / AVOID verdict with full macros, judged against your diet, goals, conditions, and allergies.

A personal daily coach

Every day Vive reads your latest health and meals and gives you one to three focused nudges (hit your protein, reach 10k steps, get your Zone 2 minutes) and ticks them off the moment you do.

Your longevity score, every day

One number from four pillars (diet, sleep, exercise, and cardiovascular recovery) built from your meal log and Apple Health. See exactly what's moving it and what to do next.

Understand every night

A complete sleep score covering duration, deep and REM stages, bedtime, efficiency, and overnight recovery, with age-aware targets and a plain, encouraging read on how you slept.

Build your aerobic base

Vive tracks your Zone 2 minutes from real workout heart-rate data using Apple's own zones, so you can hit the weekly cardio that actually supports a long, healthy life.

Recipes matched to your goals

A library of healthy, single-serving meals filtered to your diet and allergies: high-protein, low-carb, quick, and more. Log any of them in one tap, no scan required.
